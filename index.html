Publications
Inflation of Finite Lattices Along All-or-Nothing Sets
(Order 14(3) (2017), 419-440)
T. Holmes and J.B. Nation
Abstract
We introduce a new way of constructing finite lattices that extends the results of A. Day and H. Reppe. We then prove that those finite lattices that can be constructed in this way using the two element lattice are exactly those called 'binary cut-through codable' by Q. Sun and S. Lee. Finally, we show that there exist infinitely many distince varieties of binary cut-through codable lattices.
Lattices of atomic theories in languages without equality
(Preprint)
T. Holmes, D. Kitsuwa, J. B. Nation and S. Tamagawa
Abstract
The structure of lattices of atomic theories in languageswithout equality is described. In particular, these lattices are distributive, and both algebraic and dually algebraic.
